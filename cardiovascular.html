About

This site shows if you are prone to cardiovascular disease(cvd). By following this guide lines it will show if you are likely to be at risk of cardiovascular disease(cvd). If you exercise, have Heart disease, skin cancer, other forms of cancer, Diabetes, arthritis, male or female, age height, weight, BMI, how much alcohol and smoking,fruit and veggie consumption, and how many fried potatoes consumed.
